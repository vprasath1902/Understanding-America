    Claim Labels

    Stable

    Unlikely to change, but still needs citation in final edition.

    Update-sensitive

    Can change with elections, policy, fees, or agency rules.

    Legal-sensitive

    Must be written as education, not legal advice.

    Local-sensitive

    Must be verified with address-specific official lookup tools.
