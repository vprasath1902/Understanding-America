    Study Guide

    4-Week Citizenship Study Plan

    A practical schedule for preparing for the naturalization interview while understanding the system as a whole.

    Week 1

    Foundations

    Read Chapters 1–7. Focus on the colonies, independence, Constitution, and Bill of Rights.

    Week 2

    Government

    Read Chapters 8–16. Learn Congress, President, courts, lawmaking, federalism, and elections.

    Week 3

    Citizenship

    Read Chapters 17–26. Focus on your representatives, daily government, naturalization, and interview day.

    Week 4

    Practice

    Use Chapters 21–25, the quiz page, glossary, and quick reference. Practice aloud daily.

    Daily Routine

    1. Read for 20 minutes.
    2. Review 10 civics questions aloud.
    3. Explain one concept in your own words.
    4. Practice one mock interview answer.
