    Constitution in plain English

    The document has three jobs.

    🏛

    Build the government

    It creates Congress, the Presidency, and the federal courts.

    ⚖

    Limit the government

    It divides power so no single person or branch can dominate.

    🇺🇸

    Protect the people

    Through amendments, it protects rights, voting, due process, and liberty.

    Amendment timeline

    How the Constitution evolves.

    1791

    Bill of Rights

    The first ten amendments protect fundamental freedoms and limit government power.

    1865–1870

    Reconstruction Amendments

    The 13th, 14th, and 15th Amendments transformed citizenship, equality, and voting rights after the Civil War.

    1913–1920

    Progressive Era Amendments

    Income tax, direct election of Senators, Prohibition, and women's suffrage reshaped democracy and government.

    1951–1992

    Modern Amendments

    Presidential terms, D.C. electoral votes, poll taxes, succession, voting age, and congressional pay.
