    Final Citation Policy

    • Use official government sources for current procedural facts.
    • Use reputable historical institutions for history and civic education.
    • Mark changing facts with an update badge in the HTML edition.
    • Avoid unsupported claims, rankings, or partisan interpretations.
    • Separate educational content from legal or immigration advice.
