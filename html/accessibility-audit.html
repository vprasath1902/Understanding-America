    Accessibility audit

    Readable, navigable, printable, inclusive.

    This page tracks accessibility requirements before public release.

    Contrast

    Titles, buttons, badges, and callouts must pass contrast checks in light, dark, and sepia modes.

    Keyboard

    Navigation, search, reader panel, quiz, and flashcards must work without a mouse.

    Alt text

    Every informative image or SVG needs meaningful alt text or accessible labels.

    Print

    Content must remain readable when printed or exported to PDF.

    Accessibility Checklist

    • Semantic headings are nested correctly.
    • Navigation landmarks are clear.
    • Focus states are visible.
    • Controls have accessible labels.
    • Decorative images are ignored by assistive tech.
    • Interactive diagrams have text alternatives.
    • Color is not the only meaning signal.
    • Large text mode does not break layout.
    • Reading mode preserves chapter navigation.
    • Print mode removes unnecessary UI.
