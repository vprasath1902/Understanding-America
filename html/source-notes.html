    Source Notes and Update Policy

    Understanding America is designed to separate enduring civics principles from facts that change over time.

    Stable content: constitutional structure, historical explanations, and general government processes change rarely.
    Update-sensitive content: current officeholders, USCIS forms and fees, election dates, and agency procedures should be verified before publication or interview preparation.
    Preferred sources: official government websites such as USCIS, Congress, the White House, Supreme Court, Pennsylvania state agencies, and Chester County election offices.

    Recommended Verification Pass

    1. Verify current President, Vice President, Chief Justice, Pennsylvania Governor, U.S. Senators, and U.S. Representative.
    2. Verify USCIS civics test version, N-400 filing rules, fees, and interview procedure.
    3. Verify Pennsylvania voter registration and post-naturalization steps.
    4. Check all internal links and navigation entries.
    5. Review print output for page breaks and missing assets.
