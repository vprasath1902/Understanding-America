    Chapter 10

    The House of Representatives

    The House is designed to stay close to the people through population-based districts and two-year terms.

    Understanding is stronger than memorization. This chapter connects facts to meaning and real civic life.

    Learning Objectives

    • Explain congressional districts
    • Describe two-year terms
    • Understand the Speaker and House powers

    The People’s House

    House seats are allocated among states based on population. Each representative serves one congressional district.

    Two-Year Terms

    Short terms keep Representatives accountable to voters and responsive to public opinion.

    Special Powers

    Revenue bills begin in the House. The House also has the sole power to impeach federal officials, which means bringing formal charges.

    Your District

    For Chester Springs, the U.S. House district has been Pennsylvania’s 6th Congressional District. Always verify current representation before an interview or election.
