## Colonial and Revolutionary History

Native Americans lived in America before Europeans. Africans were taken to America and sold as slaves. Colonists fought Britain over taxes, quartering, and self-government.

## Founding Documents and Figures

Thomas Jefferson wrote the Declaration of Independence. The Declaration was adopted July 4, 1776. The Constitution was written in 1787. George Washington was the first President.

## The Civil War

The Civil War was fought between the North and South. Causes included slavery, states’ rights, and economic issues. Abraham Lincoln preserved the Union and issued the Emancipation Proclamation.

## The 1900s

Susan B. Anthony fought for women’s rights. Woodrow Wilson was President during World War I. Franklin D. Roosevelt led during the Great Depression and World War II. Eisenhower was a general in World War II.
