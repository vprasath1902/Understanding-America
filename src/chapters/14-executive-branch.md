## More Than the President

The Executive Branch includes the President, Vice President, Cabinet, executive departments, independent agencies, military, and many federal employees.

## Cabinet Departments

Departments such as State, Treasury, Defense, Justice, Homeland Security, Health and Human Services, Transportation, and Interior handle specialized responsibilities.

## Everyday Examples

USCIS handles naturalization, the Department of State issues passports, the IRS handles federal taxes, TSA screens travelers, and the National Park Service manages national parks.

## Career Public Service

Most federal employees are career professionals who continue serving regardless of which party controls the White House.
