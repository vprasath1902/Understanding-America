## Article I Comes First

The Constitution lists Congress first because lawmaking was expected to be a central responsibility of representative government.

## Two Chambers

The House represents people by population. The Senate represents states equally. This was the Great Compromise.

## Powers of Congress

Congress makes federal laws, levies taxes, spends money, declares war, oversees the Executive Branch, and has impeachment powers.

## Committees

Much of Congress’s work happens in committees that study bills, hold hearings, and recommend changes.
