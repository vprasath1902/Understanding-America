## Constitution and Government

The Constitution is the supreme law of the land. It sets up the government, defines its powers, and protects basic rights.

## We the People

The first three words of the Constitution show that government receives authority from the people.

## Bill of Rights

The first ten amendments are the Bill of Rights. They protect freedoms such as speech, religion, press, assembly, and petition.

## Declaration of Independence

The Declaration announced independence from Great Britain and expressed rights such as life, liberty, and the pursuit of happiness.
