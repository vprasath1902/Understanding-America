## Branches and Rule of Law

The three branches are Legislative, Executive, and Judicial. Rule of law means everyone, including leaders, must obey the law.

## Congress

Congress makes federal laws. It consists of the Senate and House. There are 100 Senators and 435 voting House members.

## President

The President leads the Executive Branch, signs bills, vetoes bills, and is Commander in Chief. The Vice President succeeds if the President cannot serve.

## Courts

The Judicial Branch reviews laws, explains laws, resolves disputes, and decides whether laws are constitutional. The Supreme Court is the highest court.
