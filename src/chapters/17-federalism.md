## Shared Power

The United States has one federal government and fifty state governments. Local governments operate under state authority.

## Federal Responsibilities

Immigration, citizenship, passports, national defense, foreign policy, currency, and federal taxes are national responsibilities.

## State and Local Responsibilities

Driver licenses, education, state roads, state police, zoning, local parks, and local services are usually state or local responsibilities.

## Conflict and Supremacy

When valid federal law conflicts with state law, federal law generally prevails under the Supremacy Clause. Courts may decide whether a conflict exists.
