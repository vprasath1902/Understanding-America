## USCIS Fees (Form N-400, Application for Naturalization)

Fees are set by USCIS and change periodically. **Always confirm the current
amount** with the [USCIS Fee Schedule (G-1055)](https://www.uscis.gov/g-1055)
and the [USCIS Fee Calculator](https://www.uscis.gov/feecalculator) before filing.

- **N-400 filing fee — online:** $710
- **N-400 filing fee — paper (by mail):** $760
- **Reduced fee (household income < 400% of the Federal Poverty Guidelines):** $380
- **Fee waiver (income ≤ 150% of the Federal Poverty Guidelines):** request with [Form I-912](https://www.uscis.gov/i-912)
- **Biometric services:** no separate fee for Form N-400

Source: [USCIS — Form N-400](https://www.uscis.gov/n-400)
